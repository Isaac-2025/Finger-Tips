About Our CEO

Eric E. Gyabaa Mensah

C.E.O & Creative Director

Eric E. Gyabaa Mensah is the visionary founder and CEO of Finger Tips Multimedia. With over a decade of experience in the creative arts industry, Eric has cultivated a deep passion for transforming ordinary materials into extraordinary art.

His journey began with a simple fascination for tie and dye techniques, which expanded into a comprehensive creative enterprise encompassing graphic design, bridal fan creation, printmaking, interior decoration, and picture making.

Under Eric's leadership, Finger Tips Multimedia has become a renowned name in Ghana's creative sector, known for its attention to detail, innovative approaches, and commitment to delivering exceptional quality. Eric believes that creativity is not just about making things beautiful, but about telling stories that resonate with people and cultures.

His hands-on approach to every project ensures that each piece produced by Finger Tips Multimedia carries a unique touch of artistic excellence and personal commitment.
